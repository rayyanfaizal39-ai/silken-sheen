# Disney's 12 Principles of Animation - Skill Marketplace

A comprehensive collection of 144 Claude skills for mastering animation principles across every context, tool, and scenario.

## The 12 Principles at a Glance

| # | Principle | Core Concept |
|---|-----------|--------------|
| 1 | **Squash & Stretch** | Volume deformation shows weight and flexibility |
| 2 | **Anticipation** | Preparation signals upcoming action |
| 3 | **Staging** | Direct attention to what matters |
| 4 | **Straight Ahead / Pose to Pose** | Sequential vs keyframe approaches |
| 5 | **Follow Through / Overlapping** | Parts move at different rates |
| 6 | **Slow In / Slow Out** | Natural acceleration and deceleration |
| 7 | **Arc** | Organic curved motion paths |
| 8 | **Secondary Action** | Supporting movements reinforce primary |
| 9 | **Timing** | Speed conveys weight and emotion |
| 10 | **Exaggeration** | Push beyond reality for clarity |
| 11 | **Solid Drawing** | Maintain volume and spatial consistency |
| 12 | **Appeal** | Charisma and watchability |
