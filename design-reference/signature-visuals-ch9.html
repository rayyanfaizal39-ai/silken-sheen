💧 The Water Cycle

Actually animated — water vapour rises, drifts, and falls, instead of a static arrow loop.

The total amount of water on Earth never changes — it just keeps moving between sea, sky, land, and back again.

🪨 The Rock Cycle

A clean table — igneous, sedimentary and metamorphic rocks constantly transform into each other, never a dead end.
