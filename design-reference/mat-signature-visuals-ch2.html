🧮 Problem-Solving — HCF (Charity Packs)

"252 shirts, 180 trousers, 108 socks divided equally into packs. Find the maximum number of packs."

1
Understanding
  • 252 shirts, 180 trousers, 108 socks divided equally
2
Devising a Plan
  • Find the HCF of 252, 180 and 108
3
Implementing
  • 252=2²×3²×7, 180=2²×3²×5, 108=2²×3³
  • HCF = 2²×3² = 36 packs
4
Reflection
  • 252÷36=7, 180÷36=5, 108÷36=3 ✓

🎯 Three Methods to Find LCM

Listing Common Multiples
Multiples of 2: 2,4,6,8,10…
Multiples of 3: 3,6,9,12…
LCM(2,3) = 6
Repeated Division
3 | 3,6,9 → 1,2,3
2 | 1,2,3 → 1,1,3
3 | 1,1,3 → 1,1,1
LCM = 3×2×3 = 18
Prime Factorisation
3 = 3
8 = 2×2×2
12 = 2×2×3
LCM = 2×2×2×3 = 24

🧮 Problem-Solving — LCM (Coffee & Tea Boxes)

"Coffee: 6 cans/box. Tea: 9 cans/box. Find the minimum boxes for equal cans of each."

1
Understanding
  • Coffee = 6/box, Tea = 9/box, find minimum equal boxes
2
Devising a Plan
  • Find LCM of 6 and 9
3
Implementing
  • LCM(6,9) = 18
  • Coffee boxes = 18÷6 = 3; Tea boxes = 18÷9 = 2
4
Reflection
  • 3×6=18, 2×9=18 — both equal ✓
