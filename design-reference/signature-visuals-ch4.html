🔬 Reproductive System Reference

A clean labeled table — clearer and lower-risk than an illustration for this content.

♂ Male Reproductive System

PartFunction
TestisProduces sperm and sex hormones
ScrotumHolds and protects the testes
Sperm ductTransports sperm from the testis to the urethra
Seminal vesicleSecretes nutritional fluid for the sperm
Prostate glandSecretes fluid that protects sperm cells
UrethraDischarges sperm and urine from the body
PenisTransfers sperm into the vagina during copulation

♀ Female Reproductive System

PartFunction
OvaryProduces ovum and female sex hormones
Fallopian tubeWhere fertilisation between sperm and ovum occurs
Uterus (womb)Where the embryo develops and grows
CervixProduces mucus to enable sperm to swim into the uterus
VaginaReceives sperm; the channel through which a baby is born
